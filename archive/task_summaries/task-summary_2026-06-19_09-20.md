## Objective
Audit and compare all data claims in TaiChu Five Mountains papers against actual measured results.

## Key Reasoning
1. **Three-tier data classification**: Level A (directly citable), Level B (needs correction), Level C (needs deletion or target annotation)
2. **Critical finding**: Cosine Similarity >0.9999999 was achieved with Alpha=0 (SFA disabled), not real SFA
3. **PPL improvements are real**: -10.02% (long), -6.34% (short), -2.53% (WikiText-2) are all validated

- **Level C (Delete/Annotate)**: 4.16× speedup (never measured, mark as target)
- **Paper revision required**: Update SFA_Technical_Paper.tex with corrected data
